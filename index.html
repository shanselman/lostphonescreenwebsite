Features

  • Exceedingly simple and elegant
  • Matches your Metro Lock Screen exactly
  • Choose your own wallpaper
  • Photo Hub integration with crop!
  • Can move out of the way of the Media Player
  • Add 4 lines of custom text
  • Matches both light and dark themes
  • Add Reward if Found, In Case of Emergency, etc.
